That is because we find ourselves in the middle of troubles and we remain there, worsening more and more, day after day!

Because the change is properly hindered by our system of rewards, that gives various gifts to whom presents fake, tenuous solutions but does not leave space to shining, genuine, full and decisive ideas.


On the contrary, if a citizen would have the possibility to present directly his proposal, without passing across the closed filter of any faction, in a founding social innovations market, and to have a reward in case the organs of government, urged by this new founding, would decide to promulgate it, e new era of social progress would begin. Sufficiently motivated, the persons would engage themselves to the utmost to find the better solutions, those really good, those that, by increasing the motive of joy for everyone, make agree and happy everyone.


And they would find many!

Because, let us remember it, the intelligence gushes mainly by the individuals, while associations, gatherings, factions, groups, parties, etc. for their same constitution prefer to show the simple physical force given by the number of their members, the intellectual skills of these being used for a pure exercise of adaptation and conformism to the idea leader.
