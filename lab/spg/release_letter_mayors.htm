From the Eudaemony Laboratory
to the respectable Media

Since 1997 the Eudaemony Laboratory for Social Research and Development acts, among other initiatives, also a research, with the relative campaign of sensitization, on the demographic issue.

During this time, the Eudaemony Laboratory has produced various material offering it to everyone through its Internet site:

demographic

So the Eudaemony Laboratory has been received among members of the Scientists for Population Reduction association and of the European Pherology Organizations Confederation ("pherology" is defined as the science of the human carrying capacity of the Earth or specific parts of the Earth), receiving the interested feedbacks of numerous international personalities.

In the last days the Eudaemony Laboratory has addressed to the European Mayors a note we report in example for our specific Country in order to let to this respectable Media to inform also common citizens.

We thank for the attention, and warmly greet this respectable Media.
