In Tirana people breath everyone every year 40 kg (forty kilograms) of polluting powders. On the Alps, on the Himalaya, even to the poles: the pollution is spreading everywhere, and the hope for a good life is diminishing day after day. Doesn't more exist only a place in the world where the several "bill gates" could go to shelter themselves: how we can not decide for the rationing of fuels for street private use?

Can we avoid to rethink and self-contain ourselves to try to take a remedy for our world?
