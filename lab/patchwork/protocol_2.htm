Through its protocol number, the deliberational process of a motion can be considered in the various virtual parliaments, and its situation can be showed on a map, a patchwork composed by the votes expressed by every participant.
Through a software able to graphically elaborate the data relative to a specific motion, it will be possible to explore in different manner the map that reports the votes of the individuals of a whole society.



We can order people in several ways, in basis to the consents or the dissents, in basis to the references furnished at their subscription, for age, for geographical position, name, etc. We can also enlarge, by zooming on choosen zones, or reduce the dimensions of the map, and we can intervene where we'll find disagreement zones. By understanding the reasons of the disagreement everyone can contribute to build a wide concord of thought.
