Here we are imagining new ways to participate to social life, therefore justly a new society.

We are dreaming of a society in which the person has the possibility to propose directly and easily new ideas, confronting herself about them with other persons and at last arrive all together to a decision. Be the group a tribe of friends, a sports team, a condominium, a movement, a party, a government or simply a family.
