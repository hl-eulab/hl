THE 20TH ANNIVERSARY EDITION: A CONSECRATION OF VISION

To commemorate the twentieth anniversary of its conception, the homepage of this site has been minted in four unique digital assets. This edition embodies the enduring vision of a project which, despite the great necessity for Humanity to see it implemented, is still awaiting its full realization.

Twenty years! After all this time, after reaching out to countless individuals and fruitlessly inviting them to deploy together this extraordinary instrument—one that could profoundly facilitate understanding among people and nations—how could I not celebrate this vision, even if alone?

I do so by minting four digital works, four Algorand Standard Assets, to which I attribute a value of 700,000 Algo (seven hundred thousand ALGO) each.

May these four digital coins serve as the solemn celebration of human failure to grasp, after such a long period and so many grave earthly misfortunes, an opportunity that could have done so much to improve coexistence on this Planet.
