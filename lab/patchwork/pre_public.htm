It is a fundamental issue. Till today the roles of Public Employment has been, and are still, assigned for life to few elect people. However, being resources pertaining to the entire community, for their same origin and definition of public activities, this shuld not happen. Evidently, it is not possible that a public role is of exclusive property of someone. Therefore here we hope to make soon, effectively and finally, Public Employment really public, making a fair, wise, healthy rotation among those who wanted to carry out it and had the necessary requirements.
