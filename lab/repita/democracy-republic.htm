DEMOCRACY and REPUBLIC: What They Really Are


Democracy is a societal organization where the People rise as Sovereign and establish a Republic: a vast national co-ownership composed of codes, entities and institutions, assets, properties and resources, employment positions, powers and incomes to be cared for collectively and enjoyed equally. This can happen simultaneously, when that specific part of the res publica allows (as in the case of a public garden), or by succeeding one another pro tempore (as required in public employment, power, or role) to ensure that ownership of the asset remains with the People.

Thus it is!

Democracy is the exact opposite of tyranny: empires, monarchies, principalities, or the like. In tyranny, the People remain subjugated by those who permanently hold central power, excluding others. In Democracy, however, citizens can access their Res Publica through a specific Institution that periodically redistributes it, starting with public jobs. In this way, everyone can engage in active public life and contribute to shaping their own destiny, while society benefits from a broad perspective, enriched by the contributions of all, enabling it to overcome every obstacle unscathed.

In summary: A real Democracy and a true Republic can only exist if the People have access to an institution like a Public Jobs Bank, ensuring the regular renewal of those who manage, utilize, and benefit from the res publica.
