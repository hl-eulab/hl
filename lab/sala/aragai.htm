Oh great Mother Earth,
superb particle that wheel and fly in the infinite Space,
oh great Father Cosmos,
that all you contain, infinite and eternal,
have for us Eu an eye of care!

We know well to be part of you, Mother Gaia,
not exactly your children,
but simple little, minute emanations, waves, swift breathes on your surface.

We know well to be simple actors of your will, Father Cosmos,
very incarnation of your wishes.

We know well to be only an intermediate creation of yours
in a moment of passage from an evolutive phase to another.
We know well that one day we will disappear to make room to something more evolute,
more functional, more useful to the divine will of the Whole.
We are aware of all that
and ready to participate to the process of eternal transformation of reality.

But we pray you: have an eye of care for us,
treat us better than you have done in the past!
Treating better us Eu, you will treat better yourselves and your divine task.



Eu: "essere umano" (human being); from the ancient greek "eu" (well), prefix that puts a good, positive complexion upon every word, thing and being.

Danilo D'Antonio



Laboratorio Eudemonia
