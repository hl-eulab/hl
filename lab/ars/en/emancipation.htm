ANTECEDENTS

When the various Countries of the world became democrats, the minorities who inherited from previous regimes the assignments of the Public Employments for the course of their whole life, instead to return them to the citizens as democracy demanded, continued to keep them for themselves. With the occurred democracies, these primaries collective/public resources would have to start to be participated by TEMPORARY assignments. Their hoarders, instead, took advantage of positions of power to keep the undue privileges of the tyrannical era.


ASSUMED

Democracy stands out from the tyranny in the fact that every public role, both in the area of DECISION making (politics) and in the FUNCTIONAL one (jobs: in the educational, fiscal, judicial apparatus, etc.) must be periodically returned to the people. Every public job, from the humblest to the highest, contains a certain power and for this reason cannot be owned for life. Otherwise we fall into tyranny. ALL citizens are SOVEREIGNS and CO-OWNERS of their Res Publica. Those who have monopolized it for life did that against the principle of democracy.


EFFECTS

Due to a public apparatus mantained tyrannical, our societies have continued to develop following a distorted and overturned organizational model. Eg.: associationism has absorbed a large number of fresh human energies that should be instead directed, well prepared and at temporary engagement, within the Public Employments. And the private economic sector has benefited of much public money that should serve instead to nurture and grow the RES PUBLICA. On the contrary it has been loaded with social burdens that would have involve only the public economy/sector.


CONCLUSION

The employees for life in the Public Jobs of the democratic Countries are real ABUSIVES and must be immediately REMOVED. The Public Employment at fixed-term is instead EQUAL and FAIR. It achieves that OPERATIVE DEMOCRACY without which the PARLIAMENTARY DEMOCRACY becomes a constant chatter without progress. The democratization of the Public Employment must be done with the highest priority to allow citizens to reorganize and conduct, directly, the public activities and services in the manner they consider appropriate.

The foregoing is not a mere proposal. It is matter of awareness, a real historical discovery. The Peoples of the Earth, just becoming aware of having been betrayed and cheated for decades by the PREDATORS of PUBLIC POWERS that, once become democrat, should be regularly SHARED, can live an epocal EMANCIPATION process that will make them immediately FREE. From the Countries of North Africa to those of Europe, from South America to the Orient, a single movement let us inspire: liberate and make grow our sacred Res Publicas.


In a totally peaceful, legal and civil way, this outcome be our!
