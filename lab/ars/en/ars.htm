The Harmonic Social Rotation is a organization of base, dynamic but also stabilizing, that equilibrates the traditional tendency of the human societies to crystalize themselves in forms that, for their forced inertia, do not succeed to successfully satisfy the always different requirements of the reality, and therefore periodically destabilize themselves tragically, finally ending for ever.

The ideal civil place to carry out a Harmonic Social Rotation is our Public Administrations.



This is a fundamental issue.

Until today the roles of our Public Administration has been, and still are, assigned for life to few people. These roles are however resources that belong to our whole communities, for their same origin and definition of public activities. Therefore that should not happen.

Obviously, it is not possible that the public thing is of fact of exclusive property of some people. So here we hope and work to the aim that soon will be made public, really and finally, the Public Employment. That should be obtained through a fair, healthy rotation among everyone would get it and had the necessary requirements.
