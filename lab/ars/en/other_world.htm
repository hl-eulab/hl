THE STATE ARE US

In present human societies the most important and powerful roles of our social organization, those inside the public administration, education, fiscality, justice, order, information, health and safety, are assigned for life to certain persons, leaving the citizens DISEMPOWERED and EXCLUDED. As a matter of fact the State appears to be disjoined from Citizenry. These two entities, that for social pact should coincide, should be one only one thing, as a matter of fact are frequently at odds and in opposition, both in their aims and in their behaviors.

It is often written in the constitutions of our Countries that "SOVEREIGNTY BELONGS TO THE PEOPLE" (meaning to all the people!) but the reality is vastly different. All our Countries, all the Countries of the world, literally belong to those persons who are assumed for life in the positions of the State, whether they are executives or their subordinates. Present nations are in the hands of a real oligarchy --of a low level but spread everywhere-- that still lords and subjects in a subtle, sly manner the remaining part of the population. Nations are so prevented from a natural evolution towards what the various situational ambits indeed demand.

If we are not in agreement with our government's policies, do not lose other time: let us concentrate our energies toward the removal of that particular model of social organization that is the basis for every harmful behaviour of the governments and that supports multinational corporations and economic empires, that literally is the BACKBONE of the NEOLIBERAL STATE. Public employment for life is the deep, hidden, mean origin of the majority of Europe and world's problems, be theese in ecology, human rights, peace, economy or in any other area.

Demand, therefore, for what it is impossible to deny the absolute legitimacy: claim for a PUBLIC EMPLOYMENT at FIXED TERM, equally shared and of real common belonging. When this new social system will come to the fore, no longer, for example, State radio/tv will make continuous shameless propaganda for the government unique-thought, nor public forces (persons that today are they too assumed for life, becoming so faithful keepers of oligarchyc States) will rage against citizens rightly demonstrating, least of all a bureaucratic apparatus could exist, being fertile ground for every kind of corruption and misrule. We, the citizens, will say: the State are us!

A new society, without monopolization and exclusion, based instead on equal sharing and full participation open for ALL, will take root. On that day even such ambitious aims as to see every human being on Earth having a WORK, and therefore an INCOME, and, even more important, a POWER MINIMUM GUARANTED, will become easily attainable. Unemployment and precariousness, and any other incapacity of the governments to give an efficacious answer to the exigencies of society, will become a far memory. Because the citizens will cure their interests.

Public Employment for life, the PUBLIC CAREERISM, is the reef against which all the best dreams of mankind crush. Public Employment for life is the weak ring of an otherwise indestructible chain that binds a whole world and keeps it from achieving social progress and justice. Public Employment for life is the ring that today, in the interest of all and in a peacefully, legally and ethically unexceptionable way, we must definitively break.



Danilo D'Antonio
