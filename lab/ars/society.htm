A Democratic Society
has no State neither statals*


* public workers for life


Copyright © 21/03/40 Danilo D'Antonio - Some rights granted - original



A Democratic Society, for being a society and for being democratic, needs light structures of government inside which the people, periodically appointed to care the collective interests, can circulate without stopping themselves there. This composite unity of government is situated inside an ampler and thicker central organization witch structures are of common ownership and have the purpose to furnish the population with goods and services of primary necessity or however of fundamental importance for the civil living. Inside these structures the people to whom are periodically redistributed the public roles can circulate without stopping themselves there.

A Democratic Society has no State neither statals: it doesn't have any structure in which the people can remain over a certain period of time over which, inevitably, the lack of a fresh human renewal with consequent tired stagnation would cause every kind and level of aridity and corruption.

We should also note that the general central organization of a Democratic Society doesn't furnish only guide, nourishment and logistic support. It is exactly what unites and holds together the people. It has the purpose to avoid that the society is broken up, against everybody's interest, in thousand rivulets of useless energy, by assembling people instead towards aims of sure common interest. In order that this union rise from an intimate and spontaneous desire and not artificially or through an external imposition, the culture that it is developed in the social nucleus should remember, in correct and balanced way, the positive values of the ethical and unifying, participatory aspect of life.

Out of the central organization opens instead the field of the most free individual expression, free naturally till it will respect all and everything. It is out of the central organization that every human being can express more entirely and with the greatest intensity his/her own sensibility and creativeness, his/her own original personality. If, while inserted in the nucleus, we, even fully free to be ourselves, are however directed toward a purpose collectively defined, in the area external to the nucleus we are even more free, as we can also define autonomously the objective for which to work.

The two areas of a Democratic Society, the nucleus and the outskirts, should be capillarily communicating, to allow to every citizen to easily pass from the outside toward the center and vice versa. In this way we realize a circulation of experiences and perceptions that constantly enriches the two different parts of the whole social unity. Besides, by allowing, not by imposing but simply by furnishing to whoever wants the concrete possibility to change circle of competence, all the information relative to a subject doesn't stagnate in it, on the contrary, by flowing, could bedew of precious knowledges every angle of the society. In this way we complete the focused vision given by the specialization with the organic vision given by the systemic approach.

In a Democratic Society the people, and therefore the ideas, circulate freely. Democracy consists exactly in this circulation of people and ideas. When the circulation interrupts itself and rigid structures appear immobilizing people and ideas, democracy disappears and the society immediately begins to suffer, as much to its inside as much in its relationships with the external world. Justly, in truth, because its immobility goes literally to bang with the dynamic character of the reality. By not succeeding to grab the always mutable demands of the life, the society doesn't succeed in facing them, suffering the due consequences.

Today our Countries, like every other in the world, cannot still define themselves completely bloomed democracies. The Countries of the world, too soon defined democrats, are still like buds only just disclosed that a sudden cold can suddenly kill. All of us, that sincerely pant the social progress and love liberty, have the responsibility to modify all those situations and structures that prevent the people and the ideas to circulate and the democracy to affirm in all its potential.
