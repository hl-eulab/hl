What is the will of our planet? The will of the Earth coincides with the will of the environmentalists: who so often repeat easy slogans without having made personal observations extended both geographically and in time, or with the will of the scientists: who first erect a reckless progress and then complain about the degradation due to this same their convinced, fideistic and repeated action? We are here to find out what lies behind the huge, confusing and contrasting set of inputs from which we are daily submerged. Aware that the will of the Earth, understood as a planetary living being having its own desire and destiny, is never considered. In fact the Earth is not only that limited, superficial two-dimensional plane on which we miserable humans live.
The Earth on the contrary is a grandiose three-dimensional sphere
with its own definite and firm will. And mankind should become aware that,
not knowing what the whole Earth intimately desires, can not act so as to live well
that tract of the universal continuous transformation that directly concerns all of us.

Unfortunately this knowledge is useless to be revealed to anyone.
The Earth is making a sort of global selection and only those who love it
with all of her/himself, only those who know how to cancel their own identity
and fully blend in with it, will fully understand it and will be able to survive.
