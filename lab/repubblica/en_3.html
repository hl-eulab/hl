If there exists a Public Jobs Bank (in which anyone can register and enlist to contribute) that enables the periodic, regular renewal of the personnel managing the public affair. If this Institute is not in force, then both "democracy" and "republic," though declared, though brandished, though vaunted, are fictitious. These are forms kept confused, intermediate, spurious, by those who, enjoying undue privileges, do not wish to renounce them and employ every agency and means at their disposal—primarily educational and informational ones—to keep the People unaware and in a state of subjugation.
